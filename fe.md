# AMADEUS — Design System Prompt

## Project Overview

Design a world-class landing page for **AMADEUS**, the world's first **Enterprise Agentic Orchestration Platform for Robotic Process Automation (RPA)**.

AMADEUS is **not another AI chatbot**.

It is the intelligence layer sitting above legacy enterprise systems, coordinating Humans, AI Agents, and RPA robots into one autonomous execution engine.

Every design decision should communicate:

* Enterprise
* Precision
* Trust
* Intelligence
* Autonomy
* Observability
* Modern banking infrastructure

The visual quality should rival OpenAI, Stripe, Apple, Linear, Vercel, Anthropic, Notion AI, and Palantir.

The experience should feel like a premium operating system rather than a marketing website.

# Three.js Hero Scene

The hero scene should be fully interactive.

Include:

* Rotating wireframe Tesseract with a glowing RGB energy core
* Floating iridescent Möbius strips orbiting slowly
* Infinity-shaped energy ribbons weaving through the scene
* Thousands of tiny volumetric particles
* Bloom and soft global illumination
* Depth of field
* Subtle chromatic aberration
* Parallax response to cursor movement
* Magnetic hover interactions that gently attract nearby elements

The overall composition should resemble a living computational universe where autonomous agents continuously communicate and execute work.
